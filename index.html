Features

Built to get you noticed

Deep Job Analysis
We don't just match keywords. Our AI reads between the lines — understanding required skills, company culture signals, seniority expectations, and the specific language hiring managers look for in your field.
ATS Keyword Optimization
Every resume is structured to pass Applicant Tracking Systems — ensuring you reach human eyes, not a reject filter. Keywords are woven in naturally, never stuffed.
Impact Bullet Rewriting
Transforms vague responsibilities into results-driven statements. "Managed a team" becomes "Led a 6-person team that delivered a 30% efficiency gain." Every line earns its place.
Professional Summary Rewrite
A 3–4 sentence opening that bridges your experience directly to the role. Written to match the seniority level and vocabulary a hiring manager expects — not a generic template.
Skills Gap Analysis
Compares your resume against the job description line by line — surfacing exactly which hard and soft skills are missing so you know precisely what to address before applying.
90-Second Turnaround
Job applications move fast. So do we. Go from job posting to a polished, tailored resume in under two minutes — plus a cover letter included automatically on ATS runs.
